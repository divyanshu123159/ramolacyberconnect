Documents Required to Apply

Identity Proof (Any 1)

  • Aadhaar Card (Mandatory)
  • PAN Card
  • Voter ID / Passport

For Health Insurance

  • Age Proof (Aadhaar/Birth cert)
  • Passport size photo
  • Medical reports (if pre-existing disease)

For Motor Insurance

  • RC Book (Registration Certificate)
  • Old insurance policy (for renewal)
  • Driving Licence

For PMJJBY / PMSBY

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Bank Passbook (linked to Aadhaar)
  • Mobile number linked to bank
