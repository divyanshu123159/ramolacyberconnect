Official Government Registrations & Financial Services

Identity & Travel Documents

PAN Card

  • Primary ID (Any 1): Aadhaar, Voter ID, or Driving License
  • 2 Recent Passport Size Photos
  • Address Proof (Utility Bill/Bank Statement)

Fresh Passport

  • Age Proof (Any 1): 10th Marksheet, Birth Cert, or PAN
  • Address Proof (Any 1): Aadhaar, Voter ID, or Electricity Bill
  • Bank Passbook with Photograph

Voter ID / Ration Card

  • Proof of Residence (Rent Agreement/Bill)
  • Family Photo (For Ration Card)
  • Self-Declaration Affidavit (if required)

Govt. Certificates & Legal

Caste/Income/Domicile

  • Parivar Register Copy (Must have)
  • Land Records (Khatauni/Jamabandi)
  • School TC or Bonafide Certificate
  • Affidavit (Shapath Patra)

Marriage Registration

  • Wedding Invitation Card
  • Joint Photo of Couple
  • ID Proof: Aadhaar/Voter ID of both
  • 2 Witnesses (With their ID Cards)

Birth / Death Cert.

  • Hospital Discharge Slip/Letter
  • Gram Pradhan/Ward Member Letter
  • Affidavit for Late Registration

Banking & Welfare Schemes

Bank/PM-Kisan/KCC

  • Bank Passbook (Mandatory)
  • Land Khatauni (For KCC/PM-Kisan)
  • Aadhaar Linked Mobile Number

Ayushman / EWS

  • Ration Card (NFSA)
  • Income Certificate
  • List of Family members (Parivar Register)

Uttarakhand e-District (Apuni Sarkar)

Hill Certificate

(Parvatiya Praman Patra)

  • Parivar Register Copy
  • Domicile (Mool Niwas) Certificate
  • Aadhaar Card & Passport Photo
  • Gram Pradhan Declaration

Employment Registration

(Sewayojan / Rojgar Panjikaran)

  • All Educational Marksheets (10th to Degree)
  • Domicile (Mool Niwas)
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)

Character Certificate

(Charitra Praman Patra)

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Gram Pradhan / Ward Member Letter
  • Parivar Register Copy
  • Passport Size Photo

Pension Schemes

(Old Age, Widow, Divyang)

  • Age Proof / Husband's Death Cert. / Disability Cert.
  • Income Certificate (Low Income Proof)
  • Bank Passbook (Direct Benefit Transfer)
  • Aadhaar Card
