Life Insurance Plans for Every Stage of Life

All LIC plans are available at our CSC center in Jakhnidhar. We assist you with form filling, premium calculation, and policy issuance. Free Consultation

Term Insurance Plans (Pure Protection)

Plan 954

LIC Tech Term

Pure online term plan with high life cover at very low premium. No survival benefit – only death benefit.

Age: 18–65 yrs Cover up to 80 yrs Max Sum: ₹ No Limit
Best for: Young earners, salaried/self-employed wanting maximum cover at low cost.
Plan 955

LIC Jeevan Amar

Offline pure term plan. Available with Level Sum Assured or Increasing Sum Assured option. Flexible premium payment.

Age: 18–65 yrs Cover up to 80 yrs Min Sum: ₹25 Lakh
Best for: Family protection, loan coverage, breadwinners.

Endowment Plans (Savings + Protection)

Plan 914

LIC New Endowment Plan

Classic savings + insurance plan. Maturity benefit + Bonuses + Death cover. Ideal for long-term savings.

Age: 8–55 yrs Term: 12–35 yrs Min Sum: ₹1 Lakh
Best for: Risk-averse savers, children's future, retirement corpus.
Plan 915

LIC Jeevan Anand

Endowment plan with lifelong risk cover. After maturity, basic sum assured continues as life cover till death.

Age: 18–50 yrs Term: 15–35 yrs Min Sum: ₹1 Lakh
Best for: Dual benefit seekers – maturity + lifelong cover.
Plan 933

LIC Jeevan Lakshya

Annual income benefit to family if policyholder dies. On maturity, full sum assured + bonus paid. Children's education focus.

Age: 18–50 yrs Term: 13–25 yrs Min Sum: ₹1 Lakh
Best for: Parents wanting to secure children's education/future.
Plan 860

LIC Bima Jyoti

Non-linked savings plan with guaranteed additions. Flexible premium terms. Lump-sum maturity + death benefit.

Age: 90 days–60 yrs Term: 15–20 yrs Min Sum: ₹1 Lakh
Best for: Guaranteed return seekers, conservative investors.
Plan 936

LIC Jeevan Labh

Limited premium endowment plan. Pay for fewer years, get covered for longer. High bonus accumulation.

Age: 8–59 yrs Term: 16/21/25 yrs Min Sum: ₹2 Lakh
Best for: Those wanting shorter premium payment period with long coverage.
Plan 858

LIC Jeevan Shiromani

High-value limited premium endowment plan for HNIs. Inbuilt critical illness benefit. Guaranteed additions + bonus.

Age: 18–55 yrs Term: 14–20 yrs Min Sum: ₹1 Crore
Best for: High-income individuals wanting premium savings + CI cover.

Money Back Plans (Periodic Returns)

Plan 920

New Money Back – 20 Years

Survival benefits of 20% sum assured at 5, 10, 15 yrs. Balance 40% + bonus at maturity (20 yrs).

Age: 13–50 yrs Term: 20 yrs Min Sum: ₹1 Lakh
Best for: Periodic cash requirements – school fees, home expenses.
Plan 821

New Money Back – 25 Years

Survival benefits of 15% sum assured at 5, 10, 15, 20 yrs. Balance 40% + bonus at maturity (25 yrs).

Age: 13–45 yrs Term: 25 yrs Min Sum: ₹1 Lakh
Best for: Long-term planners with medium-interval cash needs.
Plan 943

New Children's Money Back

Survival benefits at ages 18, 20, 22 yrs. Maturity at age 25 with lump sum. Waiver of premium on parent's death.

Child Age: 0–12 yrs Matures at age 25 Min Sum: ₹1 Lakh
Best for: Parents securing child's education & marriage funds.
Plan 848

LIC New Bima Bachat

Single premium money back plan. Survival benefit at regular intervals. Full refund of premium on maturity or death.

Age: 15–66 yrs Term: 9/12/15 yrs Single Premium
Best for: Lump sum investors wanting periodic returns + principal back.

Govt-backed pension plan for senior citizens via LIC. 7.4% guaranteed pension p.a. for 10 years. Capital returned at end.

Age: 60+ yrs Max: ₹15 Lakh Guaranteed Pension
Best for: Senior citizens wanting safe, government-guaranteed monthly income.

Government Social Security Schemes (via LIC/CSC)

Govt. Scheme

PMJJBY

Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana. ₹2 Lakh death cover for ₹436/year. Renewable annually via bank account.

Age: 18–50 yrs Cover: ₹2 Lakh Premium: ₹436/yr
Best for: BPL families, bank account holders wanting low-cost life cover.
Govt. Scheme

PMSBY

Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana. Accidental cover – ₹2 Lakh death/disability for only ₹20/year via bank.

Age: 18–70 yrs Cover: ₹2 Lakh Premium: ₹20/yr
Best for: Everyone! Cheapest accident cover available in India.
Govt. Scheme

Varishtha Pension Bima Yojana

Pension scheme for senior citizens (60+) administered via LIC. Assured return of 9% p.a. for 10 years from investment.

Age: 60+ yrs Return: ~9% p.a. Govt. Backed
Best for: Senior citizens wanting safe, high-yield pension income.

Documents Required to Apply

Identity & Address Proof

  • Aadhaar Card (Mandatory)
  • PAN Card (Mandatory for all policies)
  • Voter ID / Driving License / Passport

Financial & Income Proof

  • Latest Bank Passbook / Statement
  • Income Proof (Salary Slip / ITR)
  • Cancelled Cheque (for ECS)

Other Documents

  • 2 Passport Size Photographs
  • Age Proof (10th Certificate / Birth Cert.)
  • Medical Reports (if sum > threshold)
